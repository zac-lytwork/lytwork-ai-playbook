03 — Getting Started

Setting Up Claude Desktop

Claude Desktop is your command centre. Follow these steps and you'll be up and running in under 10 minutes — ready to connect your tools and start building your first workflows.

✅
What you'll need: A computer running macOS or Windows, an email address, and about 10 minutes. No credit card required to get started on the free plan.
1
Create Your Claude Account

Head to claude.ai and sign up with your Google account or email address. The free plan gives you access to Claude's full capabilities — you can upgrade to Claude Pro later for higher usage limits and priority access.

Go to claude.ai →
2
Download the Desktop App

Once logged in, navigate to claude.ai/download and download the Claude Desktop app for your operating system (macOS or Windows). Install it like any other app — drag to Applications on Mac, run the installer on Windows.

💡 The desktop app is where all the MCP connector magic happens. The web browser version doesn't support connectors, so the desktop app is essential.
3
Explore the Interface

Open Claude Desktop and sign in. You'll see the familiar chat interface plus a sidebar for Projects. Take a few minutes to start a conversation — ask Claude to introduce itself, summarise a document, or help you draft an email. Get a feel for how it responds before moving on.

4
Create Your First Project

Projects let you give Claude persistent context about your business. Click "New Project" and set it up with your company name, industry, tone of voice, and any regular instructions. From now on, Claude will understand your business context without you repeating it every time.

💡 Example project prompt: "You are an AI assistant for [Business Name], a [industry] business based in [City], Australia. Always write in a [professional/friendly/casual] tone. Our main products/services are [X, Y, Z]."
5
Consider Upgrading to Claude Pro

For daily business use, the free plan may not be enough. Claude Pro (from $28 AUD/month) gives you 5x more usage, access to the most capable Claude models, and priority availability. Most businesses find the ROI is immediate — one saved hour of work covers the monthly cost.

07 — Skills

Supercharge Claude with Skills

Skills are pre-built capability bundles that extend what Claude can do. Think of them like apps on your phone — each one gives Claude specialised knowledge and tools for a specific type of task.

How Skills Work

Skills work inside Claude Cowork and Claude Code. When you invoke a skill — for example, the PPTX skill — Claude gains detailed knowledge of how to create a perfect PowerPoint presentation, what tools to use, and how to format the output professionally. Without the skill, Claude would give you a basic result. With the skill, it produces something polished and ready to use.


Skills come bundled with Claude Cowork and can also be installed via plugins — shareable bundles of skills, MCP connectors, and tools that extend Claude's capabilities for specific industries or use cases.
